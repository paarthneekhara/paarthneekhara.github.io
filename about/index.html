About

I am a passionate coder, currently pursuing my Bachelors degree in Computer Science from IIT Roorkee. I love solving real world problems. The journeys towards the solutions have been more .

I have been recently working on developing RTIFeed, a web platform to make authentic information available to the masses. This project has kept our team inspired and motivated for the last one year. I can talk endlessly about this project and its potential, but this page is about me!

Computer Science wasn’t a planned affair for me. I started coding in 2013 and the more I explore, the more amazed I am at the sheer beauty of this subject. Machine Learning and Neural Networks in particular make me appreciate mathematics. It quenches the anxiety of my high school days when I felt topics like matrices, calculus were needless!

I love travelling and photography. They complement each other perfectly. You can find some pictures of my travels and wildlife(mostly birds) here. I started cycling a year ago and I actually wish a good night to my bike before I sleep! I love riding around my home place - Dehradun, Uttarakhand. Amidst the magnificent Himalyas, this valley is a complete paradise.
