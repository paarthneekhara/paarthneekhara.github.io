Lift Your Heart

05 January 2014


The road was misty, dull and dark,
No one was there to lift my heart.
I’m all alone in that scary way,
The road less travelled is what they say.

Was I too brave to take a try?
On the road less travelled by.
Was I too strong to face the rocks?
I want a chance to turn the clocks.

If the choice was wrong, you make it right,
Face the rocks be brave and fight.
You know you can, you know you will,
Lift your heart, you can’t stand still.

You were sane when you went ahead,
You had a thought, believe in that.
The thought was deep but wasn’t hollow,
Make the road for others to follow.
