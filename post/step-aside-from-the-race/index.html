Step Aside From The Race

19 July 2016

This is one of my first poems and my personal favourite.


Step aside from the race to think.
Relax and realize the purpose of the race
Think from an unconditioned perspective
Are we here to survive?

Why are we so desperate to keep the world moving?
Are we merely players in The Big Drama?
Being watched by an unknown audience
And we humans playing the role of fools and jokers
In the more sensible world around us.

Is this life a gift or a trap?
With luxuries merely the baits,
To catch the innocent beings
In this illusionary and deceiving world.
