Textless VC Demo

Please record audio for the following texts by pressing the Record and Stop buttons. Avoid silences in the beginning and at the end of a recording. You may re-record any number of times if you are not satisfied with your recording. You may also change the input type to select a source wav file. You may select a given target speaker or provide a custom reference audio for the target speaker. Once you are done setting the inputs, press the convert voice button to listen to the voice converted audio.
