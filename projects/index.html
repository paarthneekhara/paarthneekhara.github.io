  • Blue Water Trade Winds Pvt Ltd

    Developed marine optimization softwares BOSS and e-Therm with an small enthusiastic team of software engineers at this marine consultancy company, owned by my father.

    1. BOSS - Blue Water Optimum Speed Services

      BOSS is a ship route optimization and speech scheduling online service. BOSS models ship’s reponses to weather and relations between engine RPM, speed and weather factors. BOSS uses this model to simulate the voyage in a bounded ocean-grid indexed with weather. Based on this simulation, BOSS uses a dynamic programming algorithm to plan the most optimum route and speed schedule in the forecast weather.
