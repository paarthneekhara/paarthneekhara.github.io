Singing VC Demo

Please record the audio utterance that you want to voice-convert by pressing the Record and Stop buttons. Avoid silences in the beginning and at the end of a recording. You may re-record any number of times if you are not satisfied with your recording. You may also change the input type to select a source ".wav" file instead of recording. Next, select the target speaker for voice conversion or provide a custom reference audio for the target speaker. Once you are done setting the inputs, press the convert voice button to listen to the voice converted audio.
